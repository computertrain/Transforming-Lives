What We Do

Career Guidance

Our career guidance program helps individuals identify their strengths and interests, providing them with the tools and resources to make informed career choices. We offer one-on-one counseling, workshops, and assessments to help navigate their professional paths.

Job Readiness

We prepare individuals for the job market by offering skills training, resume writing workshops, and interview preparation. Our goal is to equip participants with the confidence and skills needed to secure employment and succeed in their careers.

Sustainable Livelihood

Our sustainable livelihood initiatives focus on helping communities develop income-generating activities. We provide training in entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and resource management to empower individuals to create sustainable futures for themselves and their families.

Skills Development

We offer a variety of skill development programs tailored to meet the needs of the community. From technical training to vocational courses, we aim to enhance the skill sets of participants, improving their employability and economic prospects.

Rehabilitation and Integration

Our rehabilitation and reintegration services support individuals who have faced challenges in their lives, helping them reintegrate into society. We provide counseling, support groups, and access to resources to promote healing and personal growth.

Victim Support

We offer comprehensive support services for victims of violence and abuse. Our trained professionals provide counseling, legal assistance, and advocacy to empower survivors and help them reclaim their lives.

Community Mobilization

Through community mobilization efforts, we engage and empower local communities to work together towards common goals. We facilitate workshops, events, and discussions that foster collaboration, awareness, and active participation in community development.
